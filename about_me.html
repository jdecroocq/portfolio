Overview

My name is Jean, a young French artist with a strong passion for cinema, photography, aviation, design, and music. 3D is at the core of my creative work, both through animation and still image production.

I started early, at the age of 9, with SketchUp, and never stopped exploring this field. Today, I’ve built a personal workflow mainly based on Blender and DaVinci Resolve, which I use to carry out my entire projects.

I work independently, managing every stage of the process in my free time. This approach allows me to deepen each aspect of production and shape a coherent visual identity from the first concept to the final image. My work often focuses on cinematic animations, with a particular passion for animating vehicles and creating beautiful landscapes, and a strong interest in photorealism, which I constantly strive to refine. Alongside this, my academic path has led me to explore a more technical dimension of 3D through CAD and industrial modeling, especially using SolidWorks, which offers a structured and precise approach to form and function.

Today, I aim to keep evolving at the intersection of visual storytelling and technical design. Feel free to check out my work on YouTube. I hope you’ll enjoy it!
